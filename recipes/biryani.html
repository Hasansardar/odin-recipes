Biryani Recipe

Description

Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices. Cook rice with aromatic spices. Fry onions until golden. Cook marinated chicken, layer with rice, onions, and herbs. Dum cook until rice is tender. Garnish and serve hot. Adjust spices to taste. Enjoy Chicken Biryani!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4-5 cups water
  • 2 large onions
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp biryani masala

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili, and salt. Allow to marinate for 30 minutes to an hour.
  2. Cook soaked basmati rice in water with bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and salt until 70-80% done. Drain and set aside.
  3. Fry thinly sliced onions in oil or ghee until golden brown. Reserve half for garnish.
  4. Cook marinated chicken with fried onions until chicken is no longer pink. Add chopped tomatoes and biryani masala. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  5. In a pot, layer partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture. Sprinkle fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint. Repeat layers, finishing with a rice layer on top.
  6. Dum cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, covering the pot tightly to allow flavors to meld and rice to fully cook.
  7. Fluff biryani with a fork, mixing layers. Garnish with reserved fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint. Serve hot with raita or a side salad. Adjust spices to taste. Enjoy!
